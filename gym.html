Gym Program

All exercises: 2 working sets, close to failure (RPE 8-9). Warm-up sets on main compounds don't count. Supersets (A1/A2 pairs) keep sessions to ~40-45 min. Compounds stay in the 6-8 rep range for strength; isolations at 12-15 reps for hypertrophy and joint health.

Monday — Upper Body + Core (all phases, ~40 min)

# Exercise Sets x Reps Notes
A1 DB Bench Press 2x6-8 + warm-up sets
A2 DB Row 2x6-8 Superset w/ bench
B1 OHP 2x6-8 Standing barbell
B2 Pull-ups 2x6-8 Superset w/ OHP - add weight if needed
C1 Bicep Curl 2x12 DB or barbell
C2 Tricep Pushdown 2x12 Superset w/ curls
D1 Lateral Raise 2x15 Light, controlled
D2 Face Pull 2x15 Shoulder health - superset w/ lat raise
E Core Circuit 2 rounds Hanging leg raise + Pallof press + Farmer carry 40m

Wednesday — Lower Body / Race-Specific (all phases, ~45 min)

Squat and Deadlift alternate weekly (Week A = Squat, Week B = Deadlift/SL RDL). This avoids stacking two heavy compounds in one session and keeps Thursday's easy run manageable.

# Exercise Sets x Reps Notes
A Back Squat (Week A) or Deadlift/SL RDL (Week B) 2x6-8 + warm-up sets - Deadlift in P1, SL RDL from P2
B Bulgarian Split Squat 2x8/leg Key exercise — single-leg strength for mountains
C Hip Thrust 2x8 Glute power for climbs
D Single Calf Raise 2x15/leg Slow eccentric (3 sec down)
E Tibialis Raise 2x15 Injury prevention — always keep
F Banded Lateral Walk 2x12/side Glute med / hip stability
G Foot Circuit 2 rounds Short-foot + toe splay + SL balance

Friday — Full Body + Stairmaster (Phase 1 only, ~45 min)

# Exercise Sets x Reps Notes
A1 DB Incline Press 2x6-8 Lighter than Monday — supplemental volume
A2 Cable Row 2x6-8 Superset with press
B Box Jump or KB Swing 2x8 / 2x15 Explosive power — alternate weekly
C Walking Lunge 2x8/leg Climbing prep — hold heavy DBs
D Ab Wheel + Side Plank 2 rounds Quick core finisher before stairmaster
E Stairmaster 20-30 min Wk1: 20 min -> Wk4: 30 min - Level 8-12
